Chaewon Baek

I am an undergraduate student double majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Electrical & Computer Engineering at Seoul National University. I am currently a research intern at SNU Transformative Architecture Laboratory (STAR Lab), where I study origami structures and metamaterials under professional guidance of Prof. Jinkyu Yang.

I also visited Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency(JAXA) Institute of Space and Aeronautical Science(ISAS) as a visiting student, where I was advised by Prof. Hiromi Yasuda.

My research interest is in geometric approaches to physical and engineering systems, including analytical and experimental study of origami mechanics, energy landscapes, and structure-encoded sensing and actuation for physically intelligent robotic systems.
